My Evolving Almanack -- My Life Goals ↗

I would like to find out is there's a goal behind it all: What is the question the answer to which is universe? My first life goal is to understand reality.

One could deduce from that that I am meaning agnostic. Though "meaning" is a frustratingly vague word which obstructed me from making progress on this crucial topic for years. Instead, of asking: Is there meaning to life or universe? I propose we ask: Is there some higher concrete goal both for universe and life (you, or humanity overall)?

When put like that, the answer is glaringly obvious that we just don't have a conclusive answer. Our best theories of physics say that everything around us, from laptop/phone you're reading this on, to croissants and puppies came from nothing. There was a huge explosion that came from nothing and resulted in everything.

Our current understanding of reality is too shallow to answer "the universe goal question" conclusively. Just as in middle ages people had no direction of attack for understanding quantum physics phenomena, we have no direction of attack on "the universe goal question", but it doesn't have to be that way forever. We could progress, and we could potentially have a reasonable shot at answering it.

So when I say "I am meaning agnostic" I mean I don't know if there's some higher goal universe or life should follow. To claim that there certainly is one is just as big of a mistake as to certainly claim there's none (i.e. nihilism). Moreover, I claim we are far away from having conclusive answers to such questions.

One should take we are far away from answering "the universe goal question" seriously. This means that likely within your lifetime you won't get conclusive answers to that question (let's not go into ASI implication for a second). You won't have manual, and you could just do whatever you want, you won't find out if there is objectively right or wrong. You're just in this universe, able to do anything you want within the laws of physics: What one should do then?

For me the answer is two-fold. First part is trying to understand reality and hopefully universe goal question. Second, regardless of answer (or lack thereof), I just want to have good time while I'm around. I just want to enjoy my time here, and help others to do the same. We could translate it into three goals I have, and humanity could have:

  • 1. Understand reality.
  • 2. Grow physical wealth available to every living being.
  • 3. With corresponding freedom to exercise it.

When I say physical wealth I mean that in universal sense. I mean anything within laws of physics. Every living being ideally would have whole galaxies it could rearrange to their will, without impeding on the freedom of others to do the same. More importantly, I include mental wealth -- ability to control and choose ones mental states.
